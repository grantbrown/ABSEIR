ABSEIR

ABSEIR is an R package designed to simplify the specification, fitting, and use of stochastic spatial SEIR and SEIRS epidemic models. For an introduction and series of examples , you can check out the vignettes The package itself also contains documentation of all model specification functionality. Information on the installation process is available on the Wiki, and any questions, comments or suggestions can be directed to the issues page. A journal publication detailing the specification and use of the ABSEIR library is currently underway, and a preprint will be made available upon submission.
